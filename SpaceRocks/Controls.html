  • Space Invaders Description

The controls for my game are extremely basic. Use the arrow keys to move up and down and the left and right arrow keys to change the direction of the ship. Press space bar to fire your gun. I didn't put a cap to how fast you can press it so you can spam it pretty fast. I went a little light on the traction/friction physics in the game so you won't immediately stop when you let go of the keys!
